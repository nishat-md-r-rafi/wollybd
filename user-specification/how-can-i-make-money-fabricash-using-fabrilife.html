How can I make money (FabriCash) using Fabrilife?

The term “ FabriCash ” is referred to the amount you as a Fabrilife seller earn when any of your campaigned products are  sold by our website .



When you create a Fabrilife campaign you can set an additional selling price on top of the base price for your products (the price which buyers will be shown for the product). The base price is a combination of the  printing cost + garment cost + production cost + Fabrilife profit margin . When an item is sold, you get to keep 75% of the additional selling price as your Fabricash.
